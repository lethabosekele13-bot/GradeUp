About GradeUp

GradeUp is an AI-powered study companion created to help students study smarter, stay organised, and reach their academic goals with confidence. Instead of simply reminding you to study, GradeUp builds a personalised learning experience based on your subjects, target marks, and study schedule.

With intelligent study planning, daily motivation, progress tracking, and guided study sessions, GradeUp helps you build consistent study habits that lead to long-term success. Version 2.0 also introduces the GradeUp AI Tutor, giving students instant academic support whenever they need it.

What GradeUp Offers

📚 Personalised study plans based on your subjects

🎯 Subject goal and target tracking

📅 Smart study scheduling

📈 Progress and completed session tracking

💡 Daily study insights and motivation

🤖 AI Tutor for learning support (Version 2.0)
